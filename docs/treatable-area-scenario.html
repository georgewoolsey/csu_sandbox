Section 4 Treatable Area Scenario

Constraint levels from least flexible to most flexible in interpretation and implementation:

  1. Forested - forested area based on NLCD (2019) classification at 30m
  2. Protected - GAP Status 1 and inventoried roadless areas (IRA)
  3. Slope > 35% - based on DEM at 10m
  4. Administrative - USFWS sensitive species habitat and GAP Status 2
  5. Riparian Buffer - buffer of 100 ft. on streams and waterbodies based on NHD water network
  6. Distance from Road > 2000ft - based on road and trail data layers
